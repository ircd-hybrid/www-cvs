CVS ::

We offer three methods for accessing the IRCD-Hybrid CVS repository: snapshot tarballs, CVSWeb, and AnonCVS. The snapshots are generated hourly, and include ChangeLogs. CVSWeb & AnonCVS are updated every 2 minutes. Use CVS versions at your own risk! CVS versions are under active development and usually are not ready for release--they may not even compile at any given moment. You have been warned!

AnonCVS instructions:

Login to AnonCVS (all versions):
  cvs -d:pserver:anoncvs@cvs.ircd-hybrid.org:/cvsroot login
For 7.2.x (CURRENT):
  cvs -z3 -d:pserver:anoncvs@cvs.ircd-hybrid.org:/cvsroot co -P ircd-hybrid
For 7.1.x (STABLE):
  cvs -z3 -d:pserver:anoncvs@cvs.ircd-hybrid.org:/cvsroot co -rHYBRID_7_1 -P ircd-hybrid

Remember, these CVS versions are not suitable for production servers!
