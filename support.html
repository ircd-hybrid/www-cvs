Support ::

Should you encounter any problems with your IRCD-Hybrid installation, you have two options for support. One option is the IRCD-Hybrid forums, and the other is the IRCD-Hybrid mailing list. Please be aware it may take some time to get a response to your question or problem.

IRCD-Hybrid Forums: http://forum.ircd-hybrid.org/
The forum supports an RSS feed too.

If you wish to subscribe to the IRCD-Hybrid Mailing List:
https://lists.ircd-hybrid.org/mailman/listinfo/hybrid

SVN commit messages (ChangeLog notification via e-mail):
https://lists.ircd-hybrid.org/mailman/listinfo/hybrid-commits

IRC contact: #ircd-coders on irc.ircd-hybrid.org

Also if you encounter bugs of ANY kind, please report them to bugs@ircd-hybrid.org so that we can resolve them as soon as possible.
